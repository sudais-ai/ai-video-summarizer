Design a **modern, interactive web application** for an **AI Video Summarization & Quiz Generation Tool**. The web app should have a **professional yet visually engaging look** suitable for students, teachers, and e-learning platforms. Follow these specifications carefully:

1️⃣ **Overall Style & Theme:**
- Modern, clean, intuitive UI
- Colors: #1A73E8 (primary blue), #FFFFFF (white), #F5F5F5 (light grey background), #FF9800 (accent), #4CAF50 (success), #E53935 (error)
- Typography: Use **Poppins** for headings (bold, clean), **Roboto** for body text (readable)
- Smooth rounded corners (8px–12px)
- Soft shadows for cards and buttons (like Google Material style)
- Animated hover effects on buttons (scale 1.05, shadow intensifies)
- Subtle micro-interactions for loading, hovering, and button clicks
- Background: Light gradient (#F5F5F5 → #FFFFFF) with animated subtle abstract shapes moving slowly (like floating bubbles or soft lines)
- Include modern **SVG icons** for AI, video, quiz, and analytics

2️⃣ **Landing Page / Home Screen:**
- Header: Logo left, navigation right (Home, Features, Pricing, FAQ, Contact)
- Hero Section:
  - Catchy headline: "Transform Any Lecture Into Summaries & Quizzes Instantly"
  - Subtext: "Paste your video link and let AI generate concise summaries and engaging quizzes automatically."
  - Animated CTA button: "Generate Summary & Quiz" (hover glow effect)
  - Illustration on right: Students watching a video on laptop, AI spark or magic effect above screen, glowing quiz cards popping out
- Hero background: Subtle animated gradient or geometric shapes
- Optional Lottie animation for AI spark effect

3️⃣ **Video Input Section:**
- Simple card layout
- Input field: "Paste Video URL here..." with placeholder text
- Button: "Analyze Video" (primary blue with subtle hover animation)
- Tooltip icon: Info about supported video links (YouTube, Vimeo, Zoom recordings)
- Progress animation: Circular loader with AI spark animation while processing
- Smooth transition from input → summary view

4️⃣ **Summary Section:**
- Card layout with clean typography
- Heading: "AI Summary"
- Summary text: 3–5 key bullet points, expandable "Read More" toggle
- Background: White card with soft shadow, subtle animation when content loads
- Animated highlight on key phrases (yellow glow on hover)
- Option to **download summary as PDF** (button with hover animation)

5️⃣ **Quiz Generation Section:**
- Card layout: "Generated Quiz"
- Each question displayed as a card with:
  - Question text
  - Multiple choice options (radio buttons)
  - Correct answer highlight on selection (green highlight with smooth transition)
- Include **animated confetti effect** on correct answers (optional small GIF or canvas animation)
- Button: "Download Quiz" or "Export to PDF" (smooth gradient hover)
- Responsive design: Cards stack vertically on mobile, grid layout on desktop

6️⃣ **Additional Features Section:**
- Stats Cards: "Videos Processed", "Questions Generated", "Time Saved"
- Each card has **icon + animated counter** (numbers counting up on load)
- Smooth scroll animation to stats

7️⃣ **Footer:**
- Background: #1A73E8 (primary blue)
- Text: White, small, readable
- Links: About, Contact, Privacy Policy, Terms
- Social icons with hover glow effect (Twitter, LinkedIn, GitHub)
- Minimal animated background shapes (optional)

8️⃣ **Animations & Micro-Interactions:**
- Smooth fade-in for sections
- Hover effects on buttons and cards
- Subtle motion on background shapes
- Loader animation when AI processes video (spinning cube or circular waveform)
- Quiz correct answer: small confetti pop or glow animation

9️⃣ **Fonts, Colors & Style Summary:**
- Fonts: **Poppins Bold** (headings), **Roboto Regular** (body)
- Primary Color: #1A73E8
- Secondary / Accent: #FF9800, #4CAF50, #E53935
- Background gradient: #F5F5F5 → #FFFFFF
- Card shadows: soft (#00000020), rounded corners
- Buttons: animated hover scaling (1.05x) with shadow glow
- Animations: fade-in, hover scale, background subtle motion

10️⃣ **Responsiveness:**
- Fully responsive: Mobile, Tablet, Desktop
- Interactive components adjust layout for smaller screens
- Mobile: Vertical stacking of hero illustration and text, quiz cards scrollable horizontally

11️⃣ **Optional Fun Details:**
- AI “spark” or glow icon near summary and quiz
- Lottie animations for hero illustration
- Confetti / pop animation on quiz completion
- Subtle micro-interactions for input focus, button clicks, and card hover

**Goal:** Make this web app **look professional, modern, engaging, and visually impressive**, while clearly demonstrating the **AI Video Summarization + Quiz Generation functionality**. The design should feel **intuitive, futuristic, and trustworthy**, appealing to students, teachers, and e-learning platforms.
